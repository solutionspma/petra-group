KEVIN — SAMSUNG GALAXY S24 (ANDROID) QUICK SHEET
================================================

Work email on a Galaxy S24 (or any modern Android phone)

1. Install **Microsoft Outlook** from the Google Play Store (often the fastest path for Microsoft 365 / Exchange).
2. Open Outlook → **Add account** → enter your **TPG work email** → sign in (complete MFA if your organization uses it).
3. If the app asks for **server** or **manual** settings, use the hostnames and ports from your IT team or from the **Guided setup** / **step-by-step** links in the Document Depot (they read the same published mail settings your company uses elsewhere).

Samsung Email (alternative)
---------------------------
Settings → Accounts and backup → Accounts → Add account → **Exchange** (wording may vary by Android version). Use the same server details as above.

If setup fails
--------------
Confirm Wi‑Fi or data, correct email address, MFA method, and whether your tenant requires **modern authentication**. Screenshot any error for technical support.
