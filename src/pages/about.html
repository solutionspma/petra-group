About The Petra Group

Over 30 years of expert insurance and financial services, dedicated to protecting your future and helping you make informed decisions about your benefits.

Our Mission

The Petra Group bridges the critical gap between employers, employees, and insurance carriers through expert benefits communication, comprehensive administration, and personalized financial planning.

We are committed to our clients and believe in long-term relationships built on trust. We provide valuable and dependable services with efficiency, integrity, professionalism, and honesty. To the employees of our clients, we work hard to communicate and educate various enrollment options so they can make informed decisions.

Our objective is to be recognized as the best in each and every area in which we do business.

Our Heritage

Founded in 1994 under the leadership of Troy J. Dixon, The Petra Group originated from a vision to provide innovative benefits solutions and personalized financial planning.

With over 40 years of insurance industry expertise, we have consistently delivered effective benefits administration to employers and financial security strategies to individuals. Troy began his career in 1984 with Life of Georgia Insurance Company, later joining Metropolitan Life, before establishing The Petra Group in 1994.

Over the decades, we've expanded our reach across Louisiana, Mississippi, and Alabama, serving hundreds of employers and thousands of individuals. We manage benefit programs for major organizations including the City of Baton Rouge (7,000 employees), Southern University (2,200 employees), and numerous school districts across the region.

Key Achievement: When partnering with Taylor & Sons Insurance Agency (2005-2008), we expanded into Mississippi and tripled premium volume within two years, establishing Taylor & Sons as one of Louisiana's largest benefit providers.
